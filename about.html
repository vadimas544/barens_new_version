About

Barens Group is a private investment and holding group with operating segments in Commodities, Real Estate and Venture Capital/Technology.

The Group traces its roots to1995, initially starting as a cocoa and palm oil trading business. Since then, we have been participating in strategic opportunities to generate growth over time and bring scalable, positive impact towards the communities and environment.

Purpose

Our purpose is to be one of the leading private investment & holding companies, deliver solutions that advance everyday life and maximise value for our stakeholders.
